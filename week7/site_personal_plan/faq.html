What can you do with Google Tag Manager?

With Tag Manager, you can add and modify tags to track conversions, analyze your websites, apply remarketing strategies, and much more. You have endless options to monitor the activity of your websites and applications.

How does Tag Manager work?

Google Tag Manager works with a single piece of JavaScript code that you add to your website as a container for all the tags you want to manage. When a user activates a tag (for example, by clicking a link or loading a page), GTM retrieves it and inserts it into your website code.

What is Google Ads and Google Analytics?

Google Analytics is the most used website tracking and analysis tool in the world. It integrates with other Google services, such as Ads and Search Console. With it it is possible to monitor the profile of those who access your website, most visited pages, conversions, devices, cities and other data
